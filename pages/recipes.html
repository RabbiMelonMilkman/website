Hummus
  • 200 grams chickpeas (or 450 if already soaked/canned)
  • teaspoon flat soda
  • 80 grams ice
  • 15 grams olive oil
  • 20 grams lemon juice
  • teaspoon salt
  • teaspoon cumin
  • 175 grams tahini

Cook ready chickpeas 3 hours in water

Add onion and 3-4 cloves garlic and boil for another ~1.5 hours (until peels cover the edges of the water)

Strain

Quickly blend what was in the pot with 80 grams of ice. Then add rest of ingredients.

If you want to change thickness, play with tahini and water/oil proportions.
